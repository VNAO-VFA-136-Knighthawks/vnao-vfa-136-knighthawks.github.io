The VFA-136 Knighthawks are a group of enthusiasts who fly the F/A-18C Hornet in the Digital Combat Simulator (DCS). We are part of CVW-7, a virtual carrier air wing at VNAO (Virtual Naval Air Operations).
{.h1 .text-center}
